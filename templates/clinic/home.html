Medically supervised weight planning

A healthier weight plan should begin with your health—not a trend.

Healthy weight loss, weight gain, or maintenance can be affected by nutrition, activity, sleep, metabolism, medical conditions, and current medications. Dr. Patel begins with a clinical evaluation, looks for factors that may be affecting your weight, and develops practical next steps based on your health and goals.

When medically appropriate, your consultation may include a discussion of prescription weight-management options or GLP-1 medicines. Ozempic® is FDA-approved for specific uses in adults with type 2 diabetes; it is not FDA-approved as a weight-loss medication. A prescription is never automatic and depends on diagnosis, safety, contraindications, and individual eligibility.

Internal medicine physicians care for adults and evaluate preventive health needs, new symptoms, medication concerns, and chronic conditions such as type 2 diabetes and high blood pressure. Your visit and treatment plan depend on your individual needs.
