Medically supervised weight planning

A healthier weight plan should begin with your health—not a trend.

Healthy weight loss, gain, or maintenance can be influenced by nutrition, activity, sleep, medical conditions, and current medications. Dr. Patel begins with a clinical evaluation and helps shape a practical plan around the factors that apply to you.

When clinically appropriate, a visit may include a discussion of prescription options such as GLP-1 medicines. Ozempic® is FDA-approved for specific uses in adults with type 2 diabetes; it is not FDA-approved as a weight-loss medication. Treatment decisions depend on diagnosis, safety, and individual eligibility.

Common questions

Before you call

What does an internal medicine physician treat?

Internal medicine physicians care for adults, including preventive health needs, new symptoms, and ongoing medical conditions. Your visit and treatment plan depend on your individual needs.

Does Dr. Patel offer weight-management visits?

Yes. Visits may address medically supervised support for healthy weight loss, weight gain, or maintenance based on your health history, clinical needs, and goals.

Can I request Ozempic or another GLP-1 medicine?

You may discuss medication options during a visit. A prescription is not guaranteed and requires an appropriate diagnosis, a medical and safety evaluation, and individual eligibility.

How do I make an appointment?

Call the office at (321) 255-4003. The office can confirm scheduling, availability, and what to bring.
