Medically supervised weight planning

A healthier weight plan begins with understanding your health.

Your weight can be influenced by nutrition, activity, sleep, metabolism, medical conditions, stress, and current medications. Dr. Patel starts by reviewing your health history and weight concerns, then looks for factors that may be making weight loss or healthy weight gain more difficult.

Your plan may include nutrition and activity guidance, management of related health conditions, follow-up visits, or a discussion of prescription treatment. GLP-1 medicines are considered only when medically appropriate. Ozempic® is FDA-approved for specific uses in adults with type 2 diabetes; it is not FDA-approved as a weight-loss medication. Every prescription requires an appropriate diagnosis and individual safety review.
